**Why the subtitle change matters:**

The old subtitle indexed: `limits`, `drink`, `mindfully` — generic, low emotional impact, directly signals "mindful drinking" category.

The new subtitle indexes: `hangover` (our primary niche keyword), `free`, `weekends` — all three are specific to our niche and the emotional outcome users want.

**"hangover" in the subtitle is the strategic core move:**
- High popularity search term (people actively search "hangover app", "hangover tracker")
- Low competition in the alcohol tracker App Store category — competitors ignore it
- Apple gives subtitle keywords higher index weight than keyword field entries
- Positions us in the niche before users even read the description

## 4. Screenshot Captions — Updated Strategy

> **New since June 2025:** Apple OCR-indexes screenshot caption text. Apps with keyword-optimized captions showed 22–25% boost in search visibility. Unlike the keyword field, keywords from title/subtitle CAN and SHOULD be repeated in captions.

### Design rules for caption OCR accuracy

- Position captions at the **top** of each screenshot
- Use **high-contrast text** on a solid or semi-transparent band
- Avoid decorative/script fonts — OCR may fail on them
- Text should cover no more than **20% of the screenshot area**
- Design for iPhone SE dimensions (smallest visible size)

### Recommended captions (updated for hangover niche)

| Screenshot | English Caption | German Caption | Keywords Captured |
|-----------|----------------|----------------|------------------|
| 1 — BAC chart | `See your alcohol level rise and fall in real time` | `Sieh deinen Alkoholpegel in Echtzeit steigen und fallen` | alcohol, level, real time |

| 4 — Limit alert | `Your limit. One nudge before you go too far.` | `Dein Limit. Ein Hinweis bevor du zu weit gehst.` | limit / Limit |
| 5 — Calendar | `Watch your weekends improve over time` | `Sieh deine Wochenenden besser werden` | weekends / Wochenende (repeat from subtitle ✓) |

## 6. A/B Testing Recommendations

Apple allows Product Page Optimization (PPO) — test up to 3 variants of subtitle and screenshots simultaneously.

### Priority tests

| Element | Variant A (Control) | Variant B | What to Learn |
|---------|--------------------|-----------|----|
| Subtitle | `Enjoy hangover-free weekends.` | `Great weekends start tonight.` | Does "hangover" keyword lift search → download, or does emotional copy lift browse → download? |
| Screenshot 1 caption | `See your alcohol level rise and fall in real time` | `Enjoy hangover-free weekends — starting tonight` | Feature copy vs. niche outcome copy on the hero screenshot |
| Screenshot 2 | Sober-by forecast screen | Weekend calendar with green days | Does users want to see the in-night tool or the long-term progress? |

### When to run tests

Run PPO tests for minimum **14 days** to reach statistical significance. Only test one element at a time unless using a full factorial design.
